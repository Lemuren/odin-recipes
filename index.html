ODIN RECIPES

  • Italian Chicken Marinade

    This Italian dressing chicken marinade is a super simple but delicious way to add flavor before grilling.

  • Best Hamburger Ever

    The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

  • Marinated Grilled Pork Tenderloin

    This marinated pork loin cooks up nicely on the grill. It tastes best when marinated for 24 hours.

  • Texas Chili

    This Texas chili is rich and hearty with just the right amount of heat. The beef is wonderfully tender and perfectly absorbs the smoky, savory seasoning of the pepper paste and spices.
